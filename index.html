Wellness Insights

Small steps to help manage common mental health challenges.

Understanding Stress

Stress often comes from feeling like we have too many demands and not enough resources.

Tip: Try "Box Breathing" for 2 minutes to physically calm your nervous system.

Quietening Anxiety

Anxiety usually focuses on the future. It lives in the "What if?" instead of the "What is."

Tip: Use the 5-4-3-2-1 technique to pull your mind back into the present moment.

Preventing Burnout

Burnout isn't just being tired; it's emotional exhaustion from prolonged pressure.

Tip: Set a "digital sunset" by turning off all screens 30 minutes before sleep.
